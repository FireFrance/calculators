PEA portfolio comparison calculator

This calculator lets you compare 3 example PEA portfolios that have different average expense ratios. This lets us figure out whether it's worth investing in a more complicated portfolio with lower average expense ratios, but higher brokerage fees due to the need to make multiple buy orders each month, and to sell multiple funds for withdrawal purposes.

See our blog post on PEAs for more details and examples of what the actual brokerage fees are for the providers we investigated. These same providers will be used again for this calculator.

All calculators are performed in your browser and no data that you input is sent to any server
